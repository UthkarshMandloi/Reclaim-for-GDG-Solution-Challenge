Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the AI waste analysis?

Our AI model has a 95% accuracy rate for identifying common household materials and providing relevant upcycling suggestions.

Is Reclaim available worldwide?

Yes! Reclaim is accessible globally. While our marketplace shipping is currently limited to certain regions, all other features are available worldwide.

Can I sell my upcycled creations on Reclaim?

Absolutely! Once you create an account, you can set up your shop profile and start listing your sustainable creations in our marketplace.

How does Reclaim contribute to sustainability?

By helping users repurpose items that would otherwise go to landfills, we've helped divert over 25 tons of waste in our first year alone.
